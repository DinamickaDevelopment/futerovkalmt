Information of enterprise.

Dnipro linning plant Ltd. was organized in 2011 for commercialization and development of monolithic crystalline materials and ceramic constructions.

Strategic task of the company is radical cost reduction by using inexpensive heavy – wall monolithic thick walled pipe oxide-ceramic construction for mining.

Trademark: LMT pipes. ©

Linning production is organized on territory one of the iron foundry of «Nicopol ferroalloy plant» witch is the biggest maganese producer in Europe

In “Soviet time” was widespread development with participaton of The National Academy of Scienes of Ukraine and leading expert centers for effective use manganese alloy slag in public household.

Since in early post-Soviet era this material base was stagnation. New stage of development was start in 2011 with foundation of our company and common strategy with management of «Nicopol ferroalloy plant». The fulfillment of these projects allow to improve production technology and sales satisfy customer requirements.
